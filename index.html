About Me

Data carries stories, and I love peeling back the layers to uncover what lies beneath. With more than 15 years of experience in category management for the hospitality industry, I've developed a deep understanding of how decisions, trends, and operations intersect. That curiosity naturally led me to data analytics, where I focus on turning complex information into insights that can drive meaningful change in organizations serving people and communities. I'm deeply curious about problem-solving and passionate about collaboration. I believe that data, when used thoughtfully, can lead to smarter and more compassionate outcomes.

Currently Seeking: I'm looking for full-time Data Analyst positions (remote or in the Simi Valley, CA area) where I can contribute my analytical skills to policy administration or nonprofit organizations.
